Student Procedures (Day Students)

Complete guide to get started with your college journey

Welcome to Your Campus!

Follow these steps to get started with your college journey. This guide will help you navigate through all the essential procedures smoothly.

1

College Entry

Location: Main Gate (near the parking lot)

Procedure:

  • Show your admission proof or ID at the security checkpoint
  • Collect your student badge if required
Tips:
  • Keep your admission documents handy
  • Arrive early to avoid rush hours
2

Administrative Formalities

Location: Administration Office, Ground Floor of Building 6

Procedure:

  • Submit your identity documents, photographs, and admission forms
  • Pay any pending fees
  • Collect your Student ID card and welcome kit
Documents Required:
  • Admission letter
  • Identity proof (Aadhar, Passport, etc.)
  • Passport size photographs
  • Fee payment receipts
3

Library Registration

Location: College Library, Ground Floor of Building 6 (next to Admin Office)

Procedure:

  • Apply for your Library Card
  • Learn borrowing rules and library timings
  • Get access to digital resources and online databases
Library Hours:
  • Monday-Friday: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Sunday: Closed
4

Class & Department Orientation

Location: Your Department Office (e.g., Mechanical Department, 2nd Floor, Building 6)

Procedure:

  • Meet your class coordinator or mentor
  • Receive your timetable and classroom assignments
  • Understand lab schedules and department-specific rules
Important:
  • Note down your class coordinator's contact information
  • Save your timetable on your phone
  • Understand attendance requirements
5

IT & Campus Account Setup

Location: Mech Lab (Computer Section) near the Student Club, 9th Building

Procedure:

  • Set up your college email account
  • Register on the college portal for attendance, assignments, and notices
  • Connect to campus Wi-Fi and digital learning platforms
Digital Resources:
  • College Portal: portal.college.edu
  • Wi-Fi: Campus-Student (use your student ID)
  • Learning Management System: LMS.college.edu
6

Campus Navigation

Locations to Note:

Key Locations:

Classrooms & Lecture Halls

Throughout Building 6–9

Labs

Mech Lab (9th Building), Computer Labs (Ground Floor, 7th Building)

Cafeteria

1st Floor, Building 6

Restrooms & Common Areas

Ground floor, near every building

Emergency Exits & Security Points

Marked on all floors

Procedure:

  • Walk around to familiarize yourself with classrooms, labs, cafeteria, and admin offices
  • Note emergency exits, security points, and helpdesk locations
7

Orientation & Student Activities

Location: Multipurpose Hall, 1st Floor, Building 6

Procedure:

  • Attend welcome and orientation sessions to understand college rules and regulations
  • Get information about student clubs, societies, and volunteer opportunities
  • Sign up for clubs or activities of interest
Popular Clubs:
  • Technical Club
  • Cultural Committee
  • Sports Club
  • Literary Society
  • Social Service Group
8

End-of-Day Checklist

Location: Anywhere convenient (your department or hostel if applicable)

Procedure:

  • Confirm your classroom schedule and lab timings
  • Keep contact info for administration, library, IT support, and student club
  • Make a note of all campus locations for easy navigation in future
Final Checklist:
  • ✅ Student ID card collected
  • ✅ Library registration completed
  • ✅ Timetable received
  • ✅ College email activated
  • ✅ Campus Wi-Fi connected
  • ✅ Important locations noted
  • ✅ Emergency contacts saved
